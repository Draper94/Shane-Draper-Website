Shane Draper

Shane Draper is a multi-talented content creator who graduated from West Chester University with a Bachelor's in Music Composition and Theory.

Vashta Entertainment

During his time at West Chester he co-founded the video game company Vashta Entertainment with his long-time creative partner Bret Black. The company has earned over a million downloads across four game (and more to come!).

Official Website

The Exclusive

In 2017 Shane created The Exclusive. This accomplished indie-pop group toured nationally following the success of their first single. Since then, The Exclusive has released a full length self-titled album and looking to release more in the future.
